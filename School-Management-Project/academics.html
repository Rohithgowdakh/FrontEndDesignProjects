PRIMARY


The curriculum for grades 3, 4 and 5 is an amalgamation of knowledge, skills, and attitudes as highlighted in the NEP 2020. The curriculum is designed to facilitate age-appropriate knowledge, transversal skills like critical thinking, creativity, communication, student leadership, confidence, emotional and social wellbeing, etc. through efficacious strategies. An integrated approach weaving interdisciplinary projects creates a unique fabric of learning – one that intersperses cognitive and metacognitive elements and enables our children to strive for the extraordinary. Experiential learning, project-based learning, and developing a growth mindset are key areas of focus.
