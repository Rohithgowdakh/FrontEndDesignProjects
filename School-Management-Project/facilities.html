FACILITIES

LIBRARIES

Our school library is a tranquil and resource-rich environment dedicated to fostering a love of reading and learning. It boasts an extensive collection of books, digital resources, and multimedia materials to support a wide range of academic interests and reading preferences.

SPORTS

Participating in sports or recreational activities is a life long journey that helps develops the physical and mental well-being of a child. A renowned ICSE school for sports, Sherwood High’s infrastructure provides great opportunities to young & aspiring students go the extra mile;

LABORATORY

Our school laboratory is a well-equipped and dynamic space designed to enhance hands-on learning and scientific exploration. It features modern equipment and safety measures, including advanced microscopes, analytical tools, and comprehensive workstations.

CAFETARIA

Our school cafeteria is a vibrant and welcoming space where students gather for meals and socializing. It offers a variety of nutritious and delicious food options, including fresh fruits, vegetables, and balanced hot meals. The cafeteria is designed with comfort in mind, featuring ample seating and bright, engaging décor.

TRANSPORT

A lot of memories are associated with travelling in school vans – from van lunches to antaksharis to friends from different classes getting together. Sherwood High covers 47 routes in a 15km radius. All buses at Sherwood High are of the high-tech variety.

MEDICARE

The well – equipped health clinic provides medical attention to any student when the need arises. Qualified doctors assisted by trained nurses is on duty throughout school hours on all working days providing the healing touch. A state – of – the art ambulance is always on standby for any medical emergency that may arise.

BOARDING FACILITY

Our school’s boarding facility provides a comfortable and supportive environment for students living on campus. It features well-appointed rooms with modern furnishings, ensuring a cozy and conducive atmosphere for studying and relaxing.

SUSTAINABILITY

Our school is committed to sustainability through a variety of impactful initiatives. We have implemented comprehensive recycling and waste reduction programs, and we use energy-efficient lighting and equipment throughout the campus.
